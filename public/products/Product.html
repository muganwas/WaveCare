Clarox VX-30vet A compact, field-friendly X-ray device specifically tailored for small animal veterinary diagnostics. This device integrates a cutting-edge carbon nanotube (CNT) digital X-ray tube, which allows for immediate imaging upon power-up, significantly reducing the duration of anesthesia required for the animals. Its ultra-lightweight design and low power consumption are optimally suited for mobile veterinary applications
